Fee Schedule
Classes are purchased in Blocks. 
    Standard Membership: 1 block of 10 classes = $100   Good for 8 weeks.
                                     or   1 half block of 5 classes = $65  Good for 4 weeks.
    Senior Membership: 1 block of 10 classes= $75
    Student Membership: 1 block of 10 classes= $75
    First Class is Free
    Drop in Class: $20
    Note: Couples may share 1 block, they will be charged for 2 classes when both attend.
Blocks are not refundable under most circumstances. 
You can attend during any time period. 
You can start at any time of the month or year.
Blocks are renewable after 5 or 10 classes or four or eight weeks, whichever is up first.

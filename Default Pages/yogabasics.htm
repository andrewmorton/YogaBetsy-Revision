YogaBetsy's YogaBasics DVD's: VHS Resolution-Why.

These DVD's were originally released as a set of ten VHS video tapes at a time when there was no such thing as high definition TV, DVD burners did not exist yet, and most people had no idea what yoga even was.

When it became possible, the series was formatted to DVD but some choices had to be made regarding price and video quality. These discs were intended to be used by real people that wanted to learn how to do yoga and who would actually be doing postures in awkward postions which would preclude their looking at the video itself a good part of the time. It was understood that real users would be doing more listening that watching anyway, so, it was decided to put more material on the discs at a lower price instead of using higher quality video for machines most people did not even own yet.

In the future we will probably offer them at higher resolution, but, there will be more discs, they will cost more, and no one will learn more from HD resolution than they did from the old VHS resolution.

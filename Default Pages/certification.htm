What do the workshops cover?
Workshops 1-7:  Cover postures, breathing, cleansing techniques, chanting,meditation, ayurveda, setting up a yoga business, and making your own DVD.
Workshop 8:  Teaching methodology, including principles of demonstration, observation, assisting/correcting, instruction, teaching styles, qualities of a teacher and the student's process of learning.
Workshop 9:  Anatomy and Physiology.  Includes physical anatomy and physiology (body systems, organs etc.) and energy anatomy (chakras, nadis, etc.)
Workship 10:  Yoga Philosophy/ethics/lifestyle.  Study of classic yoga scriptures (Yoga sutras, Bhagavad Gita, etc. ),  ethics for yoga teachers, living the life of a yoga practitioner, how to apply your yoga "off the mat."

What are Betsy Afton's educational background and credentials?
  Betsy has a BA in English from the University of Rochester and a Ms.Ed in English instruction from St. Bonaventure University.  She has her Hatha Yoga Teacher's Certification from the Himalayan Institute in Honesdale, Pa., as well as her ERYT 500 through the Yoga Alliance.
  She has taught a wide variety of age and ability groups over the last 30 years and has been the founder and director of the YogaBetsy Yoga Center in Olean since 2000, teaching over 20,000 hours of yoga including classes and seminars at area colleges, universities and secondary schools and businesses.
  In addition to promoting yoga and being instrumental in establishing and providing ongoing support for a lively and vibrant yoga community in Western New York's beautiful and breathtaking Southern Tier Enchanted Mountain area, she has co-starred and co-produced her own commercially available series of instructional hatha yoga DVDs.
